About

Artixios contains original software creations, external software & resources, explanatory papers, guides, and a tech blog. The name “Artixios” is derived from the morphemes of Greek words and their meanings. It essentially is a combination of Art, Skill, and Science+Technology. Hence, the goal of Artixios is to set forth the idea that the pursuit of knowledge and science is not boring or monotonous but is a beautiful human endeavor worthy of the admiration we so often give to art. To achieve this goal, Artixios is a place for contributing tools and knowledge in a simple and attractive format.
