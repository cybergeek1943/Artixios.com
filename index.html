Asphaleia Browser

Need a simple, straightforward way to provide a safer and more restricted access to the internet for your students or kids? Asphaleia Browser allows you to pick which websites you want to be allowed, and then only allows students to access the curated websites through a kiosk window. It also includes additional settings and controls if you need them. New features are in the works for even more control over the entire computer.
